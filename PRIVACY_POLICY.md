# Highlark - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 3, 2026**

## Overview

Highlark ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

**Highlark does NOT collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.** All data is processed and stored entirely on your local device.

### Local Data Storage

- **Screenshots**: When you capture a screenshot, the image data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB database
- **Annotations**: All annotations (text, arrows, shapes, colors) are stored locally in IndexedDB
- **Gallery/Recents**: Your annotation history and gallery data remain on your device
- **Preferences**: Any settings or preferences you configure are stored locally

### No Cloud Sync

Highlark does **not**:
- Send data to external servers
- Use cloud storage services
- Perform analytics or tracking
- Collect usage statistics
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies for tracking

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `activeTab` | To access the currently active browser tab for screenshot capture |
| `scripting` | To inject scripts for screenshot functionality |
| `storage` | To store user preferences locally |
| `windows` | To manage extension windows (annotation window) |
| `downloads` | To enable download functionality for annotated screenshots |
| `<all_urls>` | To allow screenshot capture from any website |

All permissions are used **only** for the extension's core functionality and **only** on your device.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally:
- You can clear all extension data anytime through your browser settings
- Go to: **Chrome Menu** → **Settings** → **Privacy and Security** → **Clear Browsing Data** → **Extensions**
- No data persists on any server, so clearing your browser data removes everything

## Your Control

You have complete control over your data:
- ✓ View all stored annotations in the Gallery/Recents view
- ✓ Delete individual or all annotations anytime
- ✓ Export screenshots as PNG files to your Downloads folder
- ✓ Share screenshots via generated links (links are generated locally, not uploaded)

## No Personal Information

Highlark **does not request or collect**:
- Your name or email
- Account credentials
- Location information
- Device identifiers
- Browsing history (except current tab screenshot)

## Security

- All data remains on your device within your browser
- Your screenshots and annotations are never transmitted over the internet
- You can trust Highlark with sensitive information because it never leaves your computer

## Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
